Cambodia University for Specialties

Established in 2002, Cambodia University for Specialties is a private higher-education institution located in the metropolis of Phnom Penh (population range of 1,000,000-5,000,000 inhabitants). This institution also has branch campuses in the following locations: Kompong Cham, Seam Riep, Battambang, Bontey Menchey, Kampot. Officially recognized by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports of Cambodia, Cambodia University for Specialties (CUS) is a coeducational Cambodian higher education institution. Cambodia University for Specialties (CUS) offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees in several areas of study. See the uniRank degree levels and areas of study matrix below for further details. CUS also provides several academic and non-academic facilities and services to students including a library, as well as administrative services.

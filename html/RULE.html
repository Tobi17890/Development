Royal University of Law and Economics

Established in 2003, Royal University of Law and Economics is a non-profit public higher-education institution located in the urban setting of the metropolis of Phnom Penh (population range of 1,000,000-5,000,000 inhabitants). Officially recognized by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports of Cambodia, Royal University of Law and Economics (RULE) is a small (uniRank enrollment range: 3,000-3,999 students) coeducational Cambodian higher education institution. RULE also provides several academic and non-academic facilities and services to students including a library, housing, sports facilities, study abroad and exchange programs, as well as administrative services.

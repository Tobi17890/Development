Royal University of Agriculture

Founded in 1964, Royal University of Agriculture is a for-profit public higher-education institution located in the urban setting of the metropolis of Phnom Penh (population range of 1,000,000-5,000,000 inhabitants). Officially recognized by the Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries of Cambodia, Royal University of Agriculture (RUA) is a very small (uniRank enrollment range: 250-499 students) coeducational Cambodian higher education institution. Royal University of Agriculture (RUA) offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees in several areas of study. RUA also provides several academic and non-academic facilities and services to students including a library, housing, sports facilities, study abroad and exchange programs, online courses and distance learning opportunities, as well as administrative services.

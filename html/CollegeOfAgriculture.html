Prek Leap National College of Agriculture

Established in 1951, Prek Leap National College of Agriculture is a non-profit public higher-education institution located in the urban setting of the metropolis of Phnom Penh (population range of 1,000,000-5,000,000 inhabitants). Officially recognized by the Ministry of Agriculture, Forestry and Fisheries of Cambodia, Prek Leap National College of Agriculture (PNSA) is a small (uniRank enrollment range: 2,000-2,999 students) coeducational Cambodian higher education institution. The admission rate range is 50-60% making this Cambodian higher education organization a averagely selective institution. PNSA also provides several academic and non-academic facilities and services to students including a library, sports facilities, as well as administrative services.

Khemarak University

Established in 2004, Khemarak University is a private higher-education institution located in the metropolis of Phnom Penh (population range of 1,000,000-5,000,000 inhabitants). This institution also has branch campuses in the following locations: Battambang, Pursat, Kampong Speu, Takeo. Officially recognized by the Ministry of Education, Youth and Sports of Cambodia, Khemarak University (KU) is a coeducational Cambodian higher education institution. Khemarak University (KU) offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees in several areas of study. See the uniRank degree levels and areas of study matrix below for further details. KU also provides several academic and non-academic facilities and services to students including a library, as well as administrative services.
